The challenge for plant operators

📉

Spread compression destroys margin

When PX–Naphtha or Benzene–Naphtha spreads compress unexpectedly, the value of your physical inventory — and your processing margin — falls with it. This can turn a profitable quarter into a loss without a single operational misstep.

🌊

Price moves are unpredictable

Crude oil swings, refinery disruptions, Chinese capacity additions, and seasonal demand shifts can move spreads by $30–60/mt in days. Without hedges, your book is fully exposed to every one of these events.

🔧

Managing hedges is a full-time job

Most plant operators don't have a dedicated derivatives desk. Monitoring SGX positions, managing margin calls, and timing entries and exits requires real-time market expertise that is separate from running the plant.

The spreads we specialise in

🔷 PX – Naphtha

The most important spread for PX producers and naphtha suppliers. Driven by Asian polyester demand, PTA plant utilisation, and refinery feedstock economics. Our flagship hedging strategy with the longest back-tested track record.

FLAGSHIP SPREAD
🔶 Benzene – Naphtha

Critical for crackers and benzene producers. Sensitive to European cracker maintenance cycles, Chinese coal-to-chemical capacity, and North American supply dynamics. High-volatility spread with strong mean-reversion properties.

HIGH VOLATILITY
🔹 PX – Benzene

Relevant for facilities producing or consuming both aromatics. Reflects the relative demand between polyester-chain and solvents/SM chains. Useful for hedging integrated aromatic plants with mixed product slates.

CROSS-AROMATIC
