Strategy — 3 SGX Aromatics Spreads

🔷
PX–Naphtha Spread
The primary driver of polyester margins globally. Highly sensitive to refinery operations, crude oil quality, and Asian textile/PTA demand cycles. The flagship Upen strategy with a fully back-tested model and verified public track record.
🔶
Benzene–Naphtha Spread
Influenced by styrene and phenol production cycles. Known for sharp widening opportunities during downstream demand surges or supply disruptions. Provides excellent counter-cyclical diversification versus the PX strategy.
🔹
PX–Benzene Spread
Cross-aromatics exposure that captures the relative value between the two key aromatic products. Provides natural diversification and stability, acting as a bridge between the two primary spread positions.
